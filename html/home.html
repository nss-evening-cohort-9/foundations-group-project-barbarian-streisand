Our Story

'It was all a dream, I used to read Beer Brew magazine. Barley, hops, and malt up in the limousine.' These were the last words of Barbarian Streisand before she closed the doors on her old life and opened the doors of her brewery. On that opening day, August 7th, 1995, a star was born. Yes, we went there. Barb gave the world the gift of great beer. There was the first brew, The Barb, a meaty trip for the senses. Minds and waistlines grew. History was made. And we have never looked back

Our Facility

It was a mere abandoned wasteland of mystery juices and computer parts. We came, we saw, we covered it up with some dirt-- you could say we conquered. We've got acres and acres of things that turn water into beer. While Jesus was more of a wine guy, we think he'd be proud. Come on down and visit us anytime!
